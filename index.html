Turb-Seg-Res Project

Segment-then-Restore Pipeline for Dynamic Videos with Atmospheric Turbulence

Notice: This website is currently under development.

Tackling image degradation due to atmospheric turbulence, particularly in dynamic environments, remains a challenge for long-range imaging systems. The Turb-Seg-Res project introduces a novel approach for restoring the videos of dynamic scenes in turbulent environments, leveraging a segment-then-restore pipeline.

Our approach separates dynamic and static scene components prior to restoration, employs camera shake compensation, and enhances foreground/background with advanced techniques. This enables restoration of most geometric distortion and enhances sharpness in videos affected by turbulence.

Stay tuned for updates, resources, and the public release of our code, simulator, and dataset.
